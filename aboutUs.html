MEAL FINDER @KMUTT

                  Created in 2019, Mealfinder@KMUTT is web application designed for students, by students, to help deal with the daily struggles of deciding where and what to eat and hopefully make the process a little more convenient. We know that university students are under a lot of stress due to academic pressure, so we want to make things slightly easier for our friends by eliminating the worries about finding a meal of choice. On our web application we allow our clients to search for restaurants/cafes near KMUTT according to their preference. We also have the option for randomizing your meal in case you are feeling extra adventurous with your dinner.
